Welcome to my website!

404: Purpose not found. Just kidd--This website is created to exhibit my photos, journey, and some random crazy thoughts.

After covid, I became really bad at expressing. Inputs are always continuiing, but ideas just stay in my head and never had a chance to come out. Even if they did come out, they are often entangled with fragments. Redundancy, omission, incoherence, lack of integration, and a sense of overloading... Wechat moments seems to be a good place to dump something, but not really. I was limited to post a maximum of 9 photos with a short description(maybe just 1 emoji). Because of this I started to get used to expressing more of little chunks, random thoughts, than expressing my reflections as a "flow". So based on all of the above, the purpose of making this website is to:

1. find the "flow" experience in writing

2. apply this "flow" in other investigations, to grow a plant, not just germinate the seed.

3. challenge myself to become naturally logical when writing, speaking, or just expressing in general

4. be curious and critical about the world

There will certainly be judgements, good or bad, from audience or myself. The world itself is made of judgements. I hope the fear of judgements will not stop me from finding the "flow", instead, encourage me to improve my writing logics.

Anyway, this is just for myself as a reference. I hope you will enjoy exploring about my adventures, and maybe finding some inspirations too!
